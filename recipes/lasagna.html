Lasagna

A photo of lasagna

Description

A simple lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces couttage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzaralle, eggs, half ot the parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
